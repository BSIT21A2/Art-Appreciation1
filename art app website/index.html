The Rationale

As a team, we’ve chosen to highlight the architectural beauty of our school and its facilities. It’s often overlooked how these structures contribute to our daily lives, despite the immense benefits they provide. Our goal with this project is to celebrate the design and craftsmanship behind these buildings, honoring the dedication and hard work of the architects and builders who brought them to life.

The first this you see




The proportions of the structure, in terms of its horizontal and vertical dimensions, as well as the yellow and blue color tones used, contribute to the building’s design being pleasing to the eye. The fact that the building complex lies in an area surrounded by trees, as well as the blue color of the open sky and sunlight elevates the overall beauty of the complex.


Since it is a large, colorful, and beautiful facade, it exudes the spirit and essence of the institution. Apart from these though, more telling stories are also visible in the banners that have been strategically placed such as the successes of the institution and serve to enhance the visual aspects of the design. In totality these aspects provide a more interesting and a more striking design.

Walking in and walking out




The dancing lights and the illuminated signs make one feel celebratory. This set of elements emphasizes the architecture and enhances the focus on the banners. It is the distribution and arrangement of banners together with the lighted entrance that ensure equilibrium during sight concentration towards the midsection.


A loud atmosphere along with the lights and colorful decorations makes the viewer instantly grab the attention. The banners recognizing the achievements have significance and fulfillment feelings.

Activity Zone




This image that takes vibrance to a community gathering in a gymnasium with bright, contrasting colors blue, yellow and green that help bring the energy to the. The slightly off-center perspective captures the spaciousness of the venue, the bustle of human activity, and the unity of participants. The cheerful decorations, string lights and balloons add a sense of charm, warmth and excitement to the scene, which passed off celebratory and inviting but also creating context to a strong, active visual narrative bursting with joy, togetherness and collective experiences.

A moment of respite




Clean plain background complements the figure and color of red counter inch and neon signs and the overall effect is a semblance of dynamism. It is quite peculiar that such an emblematic element of the Italian social setting as students is depicted and emphasized and the focus on life’s reality brings a story about interactions while invigorating the scene with relatable, warm, genuine emotions.

The informality of the architecture, together with the cheerful shades of the counter and the signs, create an ambiance characterized by optimistic energy, particular liveliness of the zone and the plurality of actively stimulating interactions and social communications.

Something Familiar




The image showcases a visually appealing school building with its blue-and-white facade, creating a sense of calm and order. The repetition of windows and railings adds symmetry, while the angled perspective enhances depth. The contrast between the building, greenery, and bright sky adds vibrancy, and the presence of students brings life to the scene. Overall, the combination of structure, color, and natural elements creates an inviting and aesthetic atmosphere.

Path to a destination




This low-angle hallway shot is simply perfect; the viewer’s eye is always drawn to the vanishing point at the end of the tunnel. The repeating ceiling beams along with the glossy floor reflect off one another giving the image a three dimensional depth and interest. The cleanness and sparse composition give the image a calm architecturally aesthetic.
